Recording requirements

  • Camera position: in the corner of the court closest to the starting position of the player being analyzed.
  • Framing: the entire court must be in view, plus the space outside the lines where players move.
  • Height: on a tripod, at least 6 ft high.
  • Shutter speed: 1/1000 s or faster (freezes the ball).
  • Frame rate: 60 fps.
  • Resolution: 4K UHD.
  • ISO: 800.
  • Length: 2–5 minutes for now.
